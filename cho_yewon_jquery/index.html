About us

Do you want to know all about famous American TV show Friends? You got it!

Friends Trivia has all the interviews, behind the scene videos, and interesting facts about Friends.

Friends has 10 seasons, and have 24 episodes each season.

All the episodes have titles that start with "The One with/where". Band "The Rembrandts" sang the opening song of this famous TV series. Lisa Kudrow, who played Phoebe, also played her twin sister Ursula in this show. The character she played in the former show had the name Ursula. Matt LeBlanc is the only one who hasn't hosted SNL. At the end of the season, Phoebe changes her name to Princess Consuela Bananahammock after getting married to Mike Hannigan, played by Paul Rudd.
